Bank M&A Advisory Services

Proprietary loan-level stress testing and pro forma modeling that gives decision makers a nuanced understanding of deal economics before they ever sit at the negotiating table.

Using our proprietary loan-level stress testing software, we produce nuanced pro forma models that form the foundation for every consolidation analysis. Our approach works closely with management to build a reasonable set of synergy assumptions and deal costs before layering them within advanced forward-looking financials. With our analysis, we calculate what acquisition multiples associate with 2, 3, and 4-year tangible book value earnback periods — vital context for understanding where negotiations stand and what deal economics truly look like.

1Proprietary Deal Modeling

Our software analyzes the target bank at the individual loan level — not just portfolio averages. This means our credit marks, interest rate adjustments, and provision forecasts are grounded in actual asset characteristics. The result is a pro forma that management can trust, not a black-box output built on industry-wide assumptions.

2Synergy & Cost Assumption Framework

We sit down with your team to map out realistic synergy captures: branch consolidations, technology platform migrations, vendor renegotiations, and headcount adjustments. We also model one-time deal costs — conversion expenses, severance, contract terminations, and advisory fees — so the board sees a complete picture, not an optimistic headline number. Every assumption is transparent and adjustable.

3TBV Earnback & Multiple Analysis

The core deliverable: we calculate what price-to-tangible-book multiples correspond to 2-year, 3-year, and 4-year earnback periods under your specific synergy and cost assumptions. This gives negotiators a clear framework — you'll know exactly where a given offer price falls on the earnback curve and whether accelerating synergy capture or adjusting deal mix changes the math. It transforms negotiations from gut-feel to data-driven.

4Scenario Sensitivity & Deal Mix

What happens if deposit runoff is 5% higher than expected? What if the rate environment shifts 100 basis points? What if you restructure the consideration mix between cash and stock? Our model lets you toggle these assumptions in real time, instantly showing how each variable impacts EPS accretion/dilution, capital ratios, and earnback timelines. Decision makers walk into every board meeting armed with answers, not questions.

5Closing Deals, Not Wasting Time

Our aim is straightforward: arm decision makers with the most nuanced understanding of transaction financials possible so that deals close and management's time isn't wasted chasing banks where the economics don't work. We've seen too many institutions spend months on a target only to discover at the eleventh hour that the numbers can't be made to work. Our upfront rigor eliminates that risk.

Engagement-Based Fee Structure
Our advisory fee is based on the target institution's tangible equity, aligning our incentives with deal completion. You receive the full suite of loan-level analysis, pro forma modeling, synergy frameworks, and TBV earnback calculations — a comprehensive package designed to take a transaction from initial screening through board approval.
